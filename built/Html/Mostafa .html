Dr. Mostafa Ashraf

Clinical Psychology | 12 Years of Expertise

About

Dr. Mostafa Ashraf is a distinguished Clinical Psychologist known for his empathetic approach and evidence-based therapy. A graduate of Cairo University Faculty of Arts, Psychology Department, he holds advanced training in cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) and trauma-informed care. His work bridges academic insight and practical care for mental wellness.
