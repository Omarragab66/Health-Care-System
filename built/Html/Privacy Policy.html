Introduction

At Code Care Hospital, your privacy is a top priority. We are committed to maintaining the confidentiality of all personal and medical information you provide to us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Personal details such as name, email address, phone number, and date of birth
  • Medical history, treatment records, lab results, and prescriptions
  • Insurance and billing information
  • Usage data from our digital platforms to help improve our services

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Providing accurate and personalized medical care
  • Scheduling and managing appointments
  • Fulfilling insurance claims and billing processes
  • Enhancing service quality, safety, and patient experience

3. Data Protection

We implement strict data protection protocols to safeguard your information. All data is encrypted and stored securely, and only authorized personnel have access to sensitive data.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not share your data with any third parties without your explicit consent, except where legally required or for medical emergency purposes.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. You may also opt out of certain types of data collection or usage.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy or our data handling practices, please contact us at: privacy@codecarehospital.com
